SMART Visa Program: A Guide for Foreign Executives in Thailand

January 20, 2026

Thailand's SMART Visa programme, launched on 1 February 2018, represents the Kingdom's most significant effort to attract highly skilled foreign talent. Designed for professionals in targeted S-Curve industries, the programme offers streamlined immigration procedures that distinguish it from the conventional Non-Immigrant B visa and work permit process.

Four Categories of SMART Visa

The programme comprises four distinct categories. The Skilled Expert visa targets specialists with a minimum monthly salary of 100,000 baht employed in a qualifying industry. The Investor category requires a minimum investment of 20 million baht in a targeted industry. The Senior Executive visa applies to management-level professionals earning at least 200,000 baht monthly. Finally, the Start-up Entrepreneur category supports founders of technology-driven businesses with endorsement from designated incubators or accelerators.

Targeted Industries

The SMART Visa covers thirteen S-Curve industries: next-generation automotive, smart electronics, affluent medical and wellness tourism, agriculture and biotechnology, food for the future, automation and robotics, aviation and logistics, biofuels and biochemicals, digital, medical hub, defence and aerospace, education development, and human resource development in science and technology.

Key Advantages

SMART Visa holders benefit from visa validity up to four years, exemption from the requirement to obtain a separate work permit, no 90-day reporting requirement (replaced by annual reporting), and the ability for spouses and dependants to work in Thailand. These advantages make the programme particularly attractive for companies establishing regional headquarters or innovation centres in Thailand.
