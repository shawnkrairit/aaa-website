Prof. Dej-Udom Krairit

Founder and Chairman, ASEAN Attorneys Alliance

Managing Partner, Dej-Udom & Associates, Bangkok

Prof. Dej-Udom Krairit founded the ASEAN Lawyers Alliance in 2013, drawing on over four decades of legal practice in Thailand and across Southeast Asia. A graduate of Chulalongkorn University and Georgetown University Law Center, he has served as President of the Lawyers Council of Thailand and holds professorial appointments in international trade law. Under his leadership, the Alliance has grown from a concept into a functioning network with publications, research, and institutional relationships spanning all eleven ASEAN jurisdictions. Dej-Udom & Associates, which he founded in 1986, has been the Thailand member firm of the International Lawyers Network since 1990, connecting to over 5,000 lawyers across 66 countries.

Governance Structure

General Assembly

All member firms participate in the General Assembly, which sets the strategic direction of the Alliance and approves new members.

Executive Committee

The Executive Committee oversees day-to-day operations, implements policies, and manages Alliance initiatives between General Assembly meetings.

Practice Working Groups

Specialist working groups in key practice areas facilitate knowledge sharing, joint publications, and coordinated client service across jurisdictions.

Alliance Secretariat

Based in Bangkok, the Secretariat manages communications, events, publications, and administrative functions for the Alliance.
