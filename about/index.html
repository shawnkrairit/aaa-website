About the ASEAN Attorneys Alliance

A premier network of independent law firms united by a shared commitment to excellence in cross-border legal services across Southeast Asia.

Our Mission

To provide seamless access to high-quality legal services across all ASEAN member states through a trusted network of leading independent law firms, enabling businesses to navigate the region's diverse legal landscapes with confidence.

Our Vision

To be the premier legal alliance in Southeast Asia — the first choice for businesses seeking reliable, expert legal counsel across ASEAN jurisdictions.

Our History

Founded in 2016, coinciding with the establishment of the ASEAN Economic Community, the Alliance was created to address the growing need for coordinated cross-border legal services as economic integration accelerated across Southeast Asia.

2015

ASEAN Economic Community launched

2016

2017

2025

Timor-Leste becomes 11th ASEAN member

2026
